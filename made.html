This is a string quartet I wrote paying homage to pieces like Ives' "Central Park in the Dark". Its 'ostinato' is harmonic in nature rather than rhythmic, in the form of a series of six dyads that repeat throughout the entire piece. It's the piece I'm most proud of writing and am planning on revisiting the style someday.
